The Sweet Story

CookieHeaven was born from one simple thing — my absolute love for cookies. There's something magical about biting into a warm, chewy cookie with chocolate chips melting in your mouth. It's pure happiness in every bite.

I wanted to capture that joy and spread it to the world through cute pixel art cookies that live forever on the blockchain. Each cookie is unique, just like the memories we make sharing cookies with the people we love. 💕

With love, Melissa 🧡
